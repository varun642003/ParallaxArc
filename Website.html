The Company

Parallax is the phenomenon where the same thing looks completely different depending on where you are standing. Two people, same moment, entirely different view.

That is what we build — products that show you the same life from a different position. The version of you that took the other path. The future self who did the work. The relationship seen through both eyes at once.

Parallax Arc is an AI studio building emotionally intelligent experiences that have never existed before. We are based in India and building for the world.
